hey, I'm Lisa.

I’m someone who feels things a little too deeply and thinks about them a little too long. This space isn’t about having everything figured out. It’s more like a quiet place where I come to sit with my thoughts, untangle emotions, and turn moments into words before they fade. Some days, it’s clarity. Some days, it’s confusion dressed as poetry. About the things we all feel but don’t always say out loud like connection, distance, longing, growth, the in-between spaces where nothing is certain but everything matters.

I don’t believe life is meant to be perfectly understood. I think it’s meant to be noticed. Felt. Questioned. Lived through, even when it doesn’t make sense. This place is my way of doing that, of capturing fleeting realizations, quiet emotions, and the kind of thoughts that usually visit at 2 AM and disappear by morning. If you’ve ever felt something you couldn’t quite explain, you might find a piece of it here.And maybe… a piece of yourself too.
